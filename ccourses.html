About the Program

IIT Kharagpur's Centre for AI is launching a certification program on "Foundations of Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence".

The program is focussed on providing a true foundation in AI skills for professionals and final year engineering students. It is designed to make you industry ready through the rigour you will undergo in our program.




IIT’s reputed faculty


Interaction with IIT alumni founded AI startups


Exceptional peers


Truly effective career Upgrade


Duration : 6 months


Features

  • 12 hours per week of contact lectures
  • 4-5 hours of self-learning time
  • 5-7 hours assignments & projects
  • 24*7 online labs
  • Industry practice workshops by seasoned industry leaders

ELIGIBILITY

  • Good knowledge of at least one programming language with good experience and proficiency in programming
  • Good mathematical maturity having done courses in basic linear algebra, calculus and probability at the college level
  • BTech or BE in any Engineering stream or M.Sc. / MCA with mathematics and programming at the graduate level
  • Working professionals / Final year students are eligible to apply
